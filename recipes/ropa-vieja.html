Ropa Vieja

Description:

Ropa Vieja is a classic and beloved Cuban dish renowned for its rich flavors and tender meat. The name "Ropa Vieja" translates to "old clothes" in Spanish, and the dish derives its name from the shredded appearance of the beef that resembles torn, tattered clothing. At its core, Ropa Vieja consists of slow-cooked, shredded beef stewed in a savory tomato-based sauce infused with a medley of aromatic spices and vegetables. The key to its exceptional taste lies in the lengthy cooking process that allows the meat to become incredibly tender and flavorful.

To prepare Ropa Vieja, beef is typically simmered for hours until it becomes effortlessly shreddable. This succulent beef is then combined with a flavorful sofrito, a fragrant mixture of onions, garlic, bell peppers, and tomatoes. The dish is further enhanced with seasonings like cumin, oregano, and bay leaves, which infuse it with a robust and slightly smoky taste. The result is a hearty and satisfying meal often served with white rice and black beans, creating a delightful combination of textures and tastes that have made Ropa Vieja a cherished staple in Cuban cuisine, appreciated by food enthusiasts around the world.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds of beef flank steak
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add flank steak and cook until browned, about 4 minutes per side.
  2. Transfer steak to a slow cooker; pour in beef broth and tomato sauce. Add tomato paste, bell pepper, onion, garlic, cumin, cilantro, olive oil, and vinegar; stir until well blended.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for up to 10 hours, or on High for 4 hours.
  4. Shred steak in the slow cooker with two forks.

Watermelon Agua Fresca

Description:

Watermelon Agua Fresca is a refreshing and hydrating Mexican beverage that captures the essence of summer in a glass. This delightful drink is made primarily from fresh watermelon, which is blended into a smooth and pulpy consistency, creating a naturally sweet and vibrant base. To enhance the flavor, a touch of lime juice is often added, which imparts a subtle tartness that balances the sweetness of the watermelon. Some recipes may also include a hint of sweetness in the form of sugar or honey, although the watermelon's natural sugars are usually sufficient to provide a pleasant sweetness.

The result is a light and thirst-quenching beverage that is perfect for hot days. Watermelon Agua Fresca is typically served chilled and can be garnished with a sprig of mint or a slice of lime to add a refreshing aroma and visual appeal. This drink is a beloved choice for cooling down during the scorching summer months, offering a burst of fruity flavor that makes it a popular and satisfying alternative to sugary sodas or artificially flavored beverages.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 4 slices lime
  • 24 fresh mint leaves
  • 8 cups ice, or as needed

Steps:

  1. Purée watermelon and water in a blender until smooth. Add sugar to taste.
  2. Cut lime slices in half. Place a half lime slice into each of 8 glasses along with 3 mint leaves. Crush with a cocktail muddler, then fill each glass with 1 cup ice.
  3. Pour in watermelon agua fresca, and stir before serving.
